We are GOMPact

GOMPact is a platform designed to connect volunteers and non-government organizations (NGOs) to support disaster-affected areas efficiently. By fostering collaboration among NGOs, GOMPact ensures that resources are utilized, reducing redundancy and maximizing impact. With GOMPact, we aim to strengthen disaster resilience and response in the Philippines by empowering communities, streamlining coordination, and making every act of help count.

know more


Mission

To connect NGOs, volunteers, and communities through a streamlined platform that enhances disaster response, maximizes resources, and drives effective collaboration.

Vision

The Front-runner for highly coordinated disaster response network that strengthens resilience, minimizes inefficiencies, and accelerates recovery in affected communities.
